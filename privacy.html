Privacy Policy for AndroidLiveSplit Last Updated: 6/26/2026 LiveSplit for Android ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information when you use our application. Information collected: Accessibility Service Data: Our app uses the Android Accessibility Service API to detect physical controller button presses (e.g., volume keys, gamepads) so you can control the speedrun timer while another app is in the foreground. We do not monitor, record, or store any sensitive information, keystrokes, or screen content. Usage Data: We do not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). How We Use Information The information collected via the Accessibility Service is used solely to facilitate the core functionality of the application: triggering split, undo, or skip actions for your speedrun timer.
